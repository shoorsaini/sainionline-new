This journal is dedicated to profile the ethnohistory of the    Shoorsaini or Saini community of Punjab whose ancestry is traced to Yaduvanshi-Tomara Rajputs of Mathura, Delhi and Eastern Rajputana.

In addition, this journal also publishes academic grade articles about ancient Indian history, Rajput history, Sikh history or any other topic of ethnohistoric significance in Indian subcontinent.
